Young artists making AWG2016 GoCards

TELE-POST Greenland has in cooperation with young and talented artists created six GoCard postcards.

The young artist are: Ivínguak’ Stork Høegh, Kristine Kreutzmann, Lisa Kreutzmann and Poul Møller whom all have attended the School of Art in Nuuk.

The artists have intrepreted the AWG2016 slogan: JOIN FEEL JUMP in their artwork and the creations illustrate the traditional Inuit Games a.k.a. Arctic Sports and modern winter sports.
