PARENTAL CONSENT FORM AND DECLARATION OF CONSENT
The youth volunteer's parent or guardian must submit a parental declaration of consent, which include the declaration of consent which all the volunteers must accept, before the youth can participate as a volunteer.
Parental Consent Form and the Volunteer's Declaration of Consent are distributed in our opening hours, Monday, Wednesday from 10am to 4 pm, Tuesday and Thursday from 11am to 6 pm at the Volunteer Center (Kuussuaq 17, B31).Â Click here Â to download the form. INSTRUCTIONS TO VOLUNTEERS WHO DO NOT HAVE EMAIL

Volunteers who do not have email can register in writing on enrolment forms. They will also be registered online in GEMS by an AWG employee. AWG employees will contact these volunteers to give further information.
Volunteers should go the Volunteer Center (Kuussuaq17, B31) or call at 382034.
